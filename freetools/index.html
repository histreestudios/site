About this tool

Histree Lead Finder is a free, manual lead-research tool. Built by the same team that runs done-for-you content and lead systems for creators and businesses at Histree Studios — this is the free version of step one: finding the right people to reach out to. Pick an ICP, generate research links, save leads by hand, score them, write your offer, generate outreach, and export or email the list.

This tool does not scrape Google Maps, use paid data APIs, send automated messages on your behalf, require a login, or store your data on a server.

Recommended workflow

Stay focused. Don't try to qualify the whole city at once.

  1. Pick one niche and one city.
  2. Generate research links and open 5–10 of them.
  3. Save only the businesses that genuinely fit.
  4. Work Hot leads first, Warm leads second.
  5. Personalize your top offers with AI before sending.
  6. Email or export your list before switching devices.
