FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before we start working together.

What is your product/service about?+
Histree Studios is a Done-For-You content agency. We handle your entire content pipeline, from strategy and scripting to video editing, thumbnail design, and distribution — so you can focus on running your business while we build your audience and generate inbound leads through organic content.
How can I get started?+
Getting started is simple: click the "Book A Call" button to schedule a free 30-minute strategy call. We'll learn about your business, your goals, and your current content situation, then walk you through exactly how we can help.
Is there a free trial available?+
We offer a free 30-minute strategy call where we'll audit your current content setup and share actionable recommendations — completely free. This gives you a genuine taste of how we think and operate before any commitment.
What payment methods do you accept?+
We accept all major credit/debit cards, bank transfers, and PayPal. For ongoing retainer clients we set up simple recurring billing. Payment terms are discussed and agreed upon during onboarding.
Do you offer customer support?+
Yes — all clients get a dedicated Slack channel with their project manager and our team. We typically respond within a few hours and for urgent matters our average response time is under 2 hours.
Can I cancel my subscription anytime?+
Yes. We work on a rolling monthly basis with no long-term contracts. You can cancel with 30 days' written notice. We earn your business every month through results, not lock-in contracts.
How secure is my data?+
All client files are stored in encrypted cloud environments (Google Drive or Notion with restricted access), and we sign NDAs with every client on request. Your brand assets and strategic information are never shared outside our team without your explicit consent.
