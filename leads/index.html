About this tool

Histree Lead Finder is a free, manual lead research tool built by the team at Histree Studios. It's the free version of the first step in our client acquisition system: finding the right people to reach out to, researching them, and keeping your leads organized.

This tool does not scrape Google Maps, use paid data APIs, send automated messages on your behalf, require a login, or store your data on a server.

Recommended workflow

Stay focused. Don't try to qualify the whole city at once.

  1. Pick one niche and one city.
  2. Generate research links and open 5–10 of them.
  3. Save only businesses that genuinely fit.
  4. Work Hot leads first, Warm leads second.
  5. Personalize your top offers with AI before sending.
  6. Export your list before switching devices.
