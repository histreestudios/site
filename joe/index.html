Process

How I solve
the reach problem.

Every engagement follows one principle: solve the problem first, get paid second.

01

Deep Research First

I watch 3+ full episodes and 5+ Shorts before writing a word. I know guest names, exact timestamps, and the moments that aren't reaching their potential.

02

5-Point Channel Audit

Thumbnails, hook timing, cold open structure, caption quality, and content cadence — benchmarked against top performers. You get this free.

03

Free Sample Edit

I re-edit 90 seconds of your existing content — cold open, captions, colour grade, emotional scoring. You see the quality before spending a dollar.

04

20-Minute Strategy Call

We align on the 90-day plan and what success looks like in measurable numbers. You talk 70% of the time. That's intentional.

05

Month 1 Delivery + Review

First episode drops within 48 hours. Month 1 closes with a full analytics review. If the numbers don't move — you walk away. No awkward conversation.

06

Long-Term Partnership

Clients stay because walking away from a compounding system doesn't make business sense. That's the only lock-in I believe in.
